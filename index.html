All There is To Know About Us..

Motivolt is a company founded with a focus on applying technology in education by simplifying and increasing productivity within any academic environment.

Motivolt's long-term mission is to create a college environment where growth and networking are limitless.

The Motivolt app will be available for download in both App Store and Google Play Store.

Motivolt plans to include features such as a connection feed, group messaging, audio and video calling, media support, calendar integration, as well as seamless integration with existing education platforms.

While the Motivolt app is still under development, we are aiming for Spring 2023.

You can support us by subscribing to our mail list to get constant updates on our progress. Follow us on Linkedin, Instagram and Twitter to watch our journey.
